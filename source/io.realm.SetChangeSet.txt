io.realm.SetChangeSet
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. _io_realm_SetChangeSet:

*Superclass:*

- **java.lang.Object (?)**

This class describes the changes made to a set during the last update. **SetChangeSet (?)** is passed to the **SetChangeListener (?)** which is registered by **RealmSet#addChangeListener(SetChangeListener) (?)** .
