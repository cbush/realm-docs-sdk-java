io.realm.Realm.Transaction
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Encapsulates a Realm transaction.

Using this class will automatically handle **#beginTransaction() (?)** and **#commitTransaction() (?)** If any exception is thrown during the transaction **#cancelTransaction() (?)** will be called instead of **#commitTransaction() (?)** .
