io.realm.MapChangeSet<T>
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. _io_realm_MapChangeSet:

This interface describes the changes made to a map during the last update. **MapChangeSet (?)** is passed to the **MapChangeListener (?)** which is registered by **RealmMap#addChangeListener(MapChangeListener) (?)** .
