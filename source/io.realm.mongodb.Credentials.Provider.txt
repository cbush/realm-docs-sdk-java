^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. _io_realm_mongodb_Credentials_Provider:

*Superclass:*

- **java.lang.Enum (?)**

This enum contains the list of identity providers supported by MongoDB Realm. All of these except **#EMAIL_PASSWORD (?)**must be enabled manually on MongoDB Realm to work.
